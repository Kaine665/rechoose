The problem

Most people don’t lack goals.

They know they should sleep earlier, start the hard task, or put the phone down. But when that exact moment arrives, the old pattern runs on its own.

  • “I should sleep earlier.”
  • “I should start this task.”
  • “I should stop scrolling.”

The gap

Motivation fades. Reminders come too late.

Plans live in calm hours. Habits break in charged seconds. By the time a reminder arrives, the scroll has already started.
