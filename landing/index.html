The problem

Knowing isn’t the problem.

You know you want to sleep earlier, start the hard task, or put the phone down. But in that exact moment, the old response can pull harder than the plan you made.

  • “I should sleep earlier.”
  • “I should start this task.”
  • “I should stop scrolling.”

The gap

Plans are made when the pull is quiet.

Old responses take over in charged seconds. That is not the time to make another plan. It is the time to act on one you already prepared.

The shift

Prepare the response. Take the step. Change the pattern.

Choose concrete actions while you’re clear. When the old pattern pulls, Rechoose helps you follow them one step at a time.
